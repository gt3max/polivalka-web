Privacy Policy

PlantApp · Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data PlantApp ("we", "us", the "App") collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using the App you agree to this policy. Questions: support@plantapp.pro.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data — your email address and a securely hashed password. Optional display name.
  • Content you create — plants you save, watering/care history, journal notes, and onboarding answers.
  • Photos you submit — images you take or pick to identify a plant or to ask the AI Doctor about a problem.
  • Location — only if you grant permission, used to tailor seasonal watering and outdoor advice. You can disable it any time.
  • Basic technical data — app version and device type needed to run the service.
  • Optional improvement data — if you turn on the in-app toggles ("Help improve Identifier/Doctor"), we keep anonymous outcomes (e.g. a photo hash and which services agreed) to improve accuracy. Off by default.

2. How we use data

  • To identify plants, provide care information, and run AI Doctor diagnosis.
  • To save your plants and send local watering reminders.
  • To create and secure your account.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

3. Third-party processors

To identify plants and diagnose problems, the photo or query you submit may be sent to third-party computer-vision / AI services that return the result — including iNaturalist, Pl@ntNet, Cloudflare, Google (Gemini), and Plant.id. Plant images are delivered via image CDNs (Cloudinary, ImageKit), and the service is hosted on Amazon Web Services. These providers process data only to deliver their function.

4. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account exists. You can delete your account and associated data at any time in the App: Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where required). We do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data via the App, or by contacting us. Depending on your region (e.g. EEA/UK/California) you may have additional rights; contact us to exercise them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.
